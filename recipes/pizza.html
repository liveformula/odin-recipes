Pizza

Pizza is honestly the one of the best hands down. No one
in their right mind refuses to have pizza in the top 3. vegan or not.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Dough
  • some flour
  • pizza sauce
  • toppings of choice
  • cheese

Steps

  1. pre heat oven
  2. oil/flour board of choice
  3. roll out dough on board
  4. puff up edges
  5. add sauce
  6. add cheese
  7. topping and a lil more cheese
  8. cook in oven for 10-15 min
