Lasagna

Lasagna is easily one of my Favorite types of food.
Even with the vegan ground-beef it is still really good.
Easily one of the best vegan option, minus cheese of course.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Ground beef of your choosing
  • 1/2 an onion/li>
  • Garlic
  • tomato sauce
  • oregano
  • Taco Seasoning
  • italian Seasoning
  • Cheese
  • Peppers

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. cook noodles
  3. cut and cook vegetables
  4. Cook Ground-beef
  5. Add seasoning
  6. Combine both
  7. get large pan for oven
  8. Sauce up pan
  9. Noodle layer
  10. beef/veggie mix/li>
  11. sauce
  12. layer and repeat
  13. Cook for 1 hour at 370
